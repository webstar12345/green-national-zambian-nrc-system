Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my NRC?

Processing typically takes 14-30 days from the date of application submission.

What documents do I need?

You need a birth certificate, proof of residence, and two passport-sized photos.

Can I track my application online?

Yes! Log in to your account to track your application status in real-time.

What if I lose my NRC?

You can apply for a replacement online through our NRC Replacement service.
