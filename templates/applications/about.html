About Us

Serving Zambia with Excellence Since 1964

Our Mission

To provide efficient, secure, and accessible national registration services to all Zambian citizens, ensuring every individual has proper identification and access to essential government services.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital identity management system in Africa, providing world-class services that empower citizens and support national development through innovation and technology.

Our Story

Since Independence

The National Registration Department was established in 1964, shortly after Zambia gained independence. For over 60 years, we have been committed to serving the nation by providing reliable identification services to all Zambians.

Digital Transformation

Modernizing our services with cutting-edge technology for faster, more efficient processing.

Nationwide Coverage

Serving all 10 provinces with registration centers in every district across Zambia.

Secure & Reliable

State-of-the-art security measures to protect citizen data and prevent fraud.
